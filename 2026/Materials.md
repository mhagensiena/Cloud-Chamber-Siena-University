<h2 id="recommended-tools" align="center"> Recommended Tools</h2>

* Drill
* Assorted Drill Bits
* Soldering Iron
* Solder
* Wire Strippers
* Phillips Screwdriver
* Measuring Tape
* Clamps
* Saw
* Utility Knife
* Infrared Thermometer (Recommended)
* Hot Glue Gun (Optional)

---

> [!TIP]
> Before beginning assembly, verify that all materials and tools are available. Having everything prepared beforehand will make the build process significantly smoother.

> [!WARNING]
> This project involves electrical wiring, soldering, sharp tools, and a radioactive source. Follow all applicable safety procedures and work in a safe, well-ventilated environment.
